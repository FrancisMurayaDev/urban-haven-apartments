About Us

Urban Haven Apartments has been a trusted name in the rental housing industry for over 12 years, providing high-quality living spaces at affordable prices. Our mission is to offer comfortable, secure, and modern accommodations that cater to a variety of tenants, from individuals to families. Located in the heart of Murang'a, Kiharu, our apartments are designed with convenience in mind. With a range of housing options, premium amenities, and excellent customer service, we are committed to making your living experience as seamless and enjoyable as possible.
